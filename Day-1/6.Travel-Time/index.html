Exercise 1

● You're driving to a destination 250 KM away.
● You average a speed of 55 KM per hour.
● Calculate approximately how long the drive will take time.
● console.log("The drive will take approximately:", time, "hours");


         
        let km = 250;

        let speed = 55;

        let time = km / speed;

        console.log("The drive will take approximately:", time, "hours");
